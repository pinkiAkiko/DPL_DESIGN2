About Us

A Legacy of Knowledge & Learning

The Delhi Public Library was established on 27th October 1951 as the first pilot project of UNESCO in India. It has grown from a small lending library into one of the largest public library systems in Asia, serving millions of readers across Delhi.

With a rich collection of over 1.7 million books in multiple languages, rare manuscripts, periodicals, and a growing digital repository, DPL continues to be the beacon of knowledge for the national capital.
